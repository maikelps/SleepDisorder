There are several algorithms in Python that can handle multiclass classification problems out of the box. Some of these algorithms include:

naive_bayes.BernoulliNB
tree.DecisionTreeClassifier
tree.ExtraTreeClassifier
ensemble.ExtraTreesClassifier
naive_bayes.GaussianNB
neighbors.KNeighborsClassifier
svm.LinearSVC (setting multi_class=”crammer_singer”)
linear_model.LogisticRegression (setting multi_class=”multinomial”)
neural_network.MLPClassifier
neighbors.NearestCentroid
svm.NuSVC
svm.SVC
